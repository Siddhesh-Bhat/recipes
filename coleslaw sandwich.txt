Ingredients:
 - 2/3 cup shredded cabbage
 - 2 onions 
 - 2 capsicums
 - 1/2 cup shredded carrot
 - 1/4 cup boiled corn
 - 3 tablespoon mayonnaise
 - 4 brown bread slices
 - Butter
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 
Directions:
 1. Cut the onions and capsicums into small pieces.
 2. Add all the vegetables into a bowl along with the mayonnaise and salt and mix well.
 3. Apply butter and put the vegetable mix prepared earlier on the bread slices.
 4. Grill if desired.
